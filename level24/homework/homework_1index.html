New Bentley Continental GT V8

Price: $238,325

The Bentley Continental GT V8 is a luxurious grand tourer that combines high performance with refined elegance. Here are some key highlights: Engine and Performance: The Continental GT V8 is powered by a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine, delivering around 542 horsepower and 568 lb-ft of torque. This allows the car to accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in just 3.9 seconds1. Design and Interior: The car features a sleek, muscular design with a distinctive front grille and LED matrix headlamps. Inside, it offers a world-class four-seat interior with options for 3D leather, new quilting, and a dark chrome finish1. Technology: The Continental GT V8 is equipped with advanced technology, including a state-of-the-art infotainment system, a digital instrument cluster, and a range of driver assistance features1. Driving Experience: Known for its smooth and comfortable ride, the V8 engine provides a more dynamic driving experience compared to the W12 variant, thanks to its lighter weight

2023 BMW M3 CS Unveiled

Price: $35,000

The 2023 BMW M4 CSL sold out in record time after it was unveiled in May of last year, and today, the M3 is receiving a similar treatment with a ‘CS’ badge that indicates more power, connection with the road, and exclusivity to match. Most importantly, it’s due in Australia following launches of XM, M3 Touring and second generation M2 and is priced from a rather tasty $249,900 AUD plus on-road costs. It’s everything you expect from a BMW ‘CS’ product, with a lightweight construction thanks to the extensive use of carbon fibre reinforced plastic (CFRP), increased engine output, unique chassis setup, special design features, and a honed driving experience that’s suitable for road and track.

2024 Nissan Skyline R35 GTR

Price: $131,000

Performance: The 2024 GT-R comes in three grades: GT-R Premium, T-spec, and GT-R NISMO. The Premium and T-spec models feature a 565-horsepower twin-turbo V6 engine, while the NISMO version boosts this to 600 horsepower1. Design: The 2024 model has received some design tweaks, including a new front fascia with a boxier look, integrated side grilles, and modern circular bumper lights. The rear has also been updated with a more aggressive look and a sharper wing design2. Special Editions: Two special editions are available: the GT-R Premium edition T-spec and the GT-R NISMO Special edition. These editions include unique features like gold Rays 20-inch forged wheels and carbon ceramic brakes
